Existentialism from Dostoevsky to Sartre by Walter Kaufmann
My rating: 4 of 5 stars

Probably my fault, but I came into this book expecting a commentary on the ‘movement’. It was, in fact, an anthology of important works instead. I’m sure it’s a good anthology from one of the foremost experts on the topic — thus the four stars — but yeah.
