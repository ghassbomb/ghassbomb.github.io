Margin figure:

Margin figures appear on the margin. Highly logical. The image title. This is the image caption. Image attribution. Very importantly, they want some text to go with them, either before or after, with only one line break.

No worries if you forgot to give your figure a label. Marginnote (either for figure or with figure inside) will be created if any one of the following conditions is met:

  1. Has type="margin; or
  2. Has caption; or
  3. Has attr.
