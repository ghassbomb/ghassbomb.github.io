My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/6543094631">4 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
Woo. Brilliant biography. It doesn't just cover Erdos and his work, but also a bunch of derivative (and fun) stories like that of Fermat's last theorem and quite a few others. It lays out Erdos's life perfectly, and also presents all its mathematics in layman's terms (well, as layman as they can get).
