This page contains my notes and exercises on various mathematics textbooks.

- Proof-writing
  - *'Journey into Mathematics: An Introduction to Proofs'* by Joseph J. Rotman
- Problem-solving
  - [***'How to Solve It'***](/pdfs/how-to-solve-it.pdf) by George Polya
- Combinatorics
  - [*'Mathematics of Choice: How to Count without Counting'*](/pdfs/comb1.pdf) by Ivan Niven
- Linear Algebra
  - [*'Linear Algebra'*](/pdfs/serge-lang.pdf) by Serge Lang (incomplete)
- Game Theory
  - *'Game Theory and Strategy'* by Phillip D. Straffin
  - *'The Mathematics of Games and Gambling'* by Edward Packel
- Geometry
  - *'The Geometry of Numbers'* by C. D. Olds, Anneli Lax, and Giuliana Davidoff
- Number Theory
  - *'Numbers: Rational and Irrational'* by Ivan Niven
  - *'Invitation to Number Theory'* by Oystein Ore
- Probability Theory
  - [*'Introduction to Probability'*](/pdfs/blitzstein-hwang.pdf) by Blitzstein and Hwang (incomplete)
